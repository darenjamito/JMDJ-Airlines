Exploring Cebu: The Queen City of the South

By Daren Jamito

The Queen City of the South

Nestled in the heart of the Philippines, Cebu is a dazzling mix of the old and the new, offering breathtaking landscapes, rich history, vibrant culture, and world-class hospitality. Known as the “Queen City of the South,” Cebu is more than just a gateway to the Philippines; it is a destination that caters to every traveler’s dream.

Nature's Bounty

For nature lovers, Cebu offers a plethora of stunning destinations. Head south to witness the Kawasan Falls, a three-tiered cascade that boasts crystal-clear turquoise waters, perfect for canyoneering and a refreshing dip. Adventure seekers can also visit Osmeña Peak, the highest point in Cebu, to marvel at the jagged green peaks resembling the Chocolate Hills of Bohol, offering panoramic views of the island. If beaches are what you crave, the island’s coastlines will not disappoint. Bantayan Island is a serene paradise with powdery white sands and gentle waves, while Malapascua Island is a haven for divers, famed for its thresher shark sightings.
